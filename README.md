SuperStore Sales Analysis & Forecasting Dashboard

This project is a simple and practical Power BI dashboard created to understand retail sales performance and future trends. Instead of working with raw data, the dashboard presents clear and meaningful visuals that make analysis easier and faster.

Project Overview

In this project, I used a SuperStore dataset to track key business metrics such as total sales, profit, and order volume. Along with analyzing past data, I also built a 20-day sales forecast to support better decision-making and planning.

Key Features

Sales & Profit Tracking:
Displays total sales ($7.71K) and profit ($121.30) in a clear and structured way.

Time-Series Analysis:
Shows monthly sales and profit trends for the year 2020, helping to identify patterns over time.

Geographic Insights:
A map visualization highlights sales distribution across different states in the US.

Product Performance:
Provides a breakdown of sales by categories such as Office Supplies, Technology, and Furniture.

Shipping & Payment Analysis:
Gives insights into commonly used shipping methods and payment modes like COD, Online, and Cards.

20-Day Sales Forecast:
Uses historical data to predict upcoming sales trends, which helps in planning and inventory management.


Tools & Technologies

Power BI for dashboard development and visualization

DAX for creating calculated measures and KPIs

Power Query for data cleaning and transformation

Built-in forecasting tools in Power BI for time-series prediction
